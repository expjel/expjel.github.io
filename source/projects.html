2018 NASA Student Launch

The NASA Student Launch is a rigorous and closely collaborative 8-month long high-powered rocketry competition among colleges and universities. Learn more about the project here!

    As a Payload Specialist on this project, I:
  • Designed and built a custom unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) payload that (1) deploys autonomously once the rocket lands and (2) delivers a beacon to a randomly placed target location
  • Delivered over 750 pages of technical documentation over the course of the project, detailing design and limitations, project management, and safety for review by NASA panel
  • Led education engagement programs within the commmunity to fundraise entire project budget
  • Completed project with a successful launch at the NASA Marshall Space Flight Center in Huntsville, AL ☺
